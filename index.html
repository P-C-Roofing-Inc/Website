Questions?

When should I consider getting a roof replacement?

-Check the age of your roof—it might be nearing the end of its lifespan.

-Look for visible damage like missing, cracked, or curled shingles, and moss or algae growth.

-Notice any interior signs like peeling paint, dark spots, or mold growth.

What is the typical timeframe for replacing a home's roof?

-The timeframe for replacing your roof varies based on its size. A smaller roof might only take a single day to install, while larger roofs could require several days to complete.
